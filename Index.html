Good to Know
Currency — Icelandic Króna (ISK)
Cards accepted almost everywhere. No need for cash except Kerið crater (~1,000 ISK / ~$12 CAD). 1,000 ISK = $12 CAD. ATMs at Keflavík airport on arrival if needed.

Driving — important rules
Speed limit: 90 km/h paved, 80 km/h gravel. Headlights on at all times (mandatory). Never drive off-road — illegal and a $5,000+ fine. F-roads require 4x4 — your Defender qualifies.
Reynisfjara Black Sand Beach
⚠️ Sneaker waves kill people every year. Stay 30m+ from the waterline at all times. Not negotiable.
Midnight Sun — June
It never gets fully dark. Bring a sleep mask. Sunrise ~3am, sunset ~midnight. Golden light at 11pm is extraordinary — stay up for it at least once.
Packing essentials
Waterproof jacket + trousers (mandatory). Layers — weather changes fast. Swimwear for every hot spring. Wool underlayers for Silfra snorkel. Good walking shoes with ankle support.
Petrol stations
Fill up in every town — stations are sparse in the East Fjords and Westfjords. N1 and Orkan are the main chains. Card payment at unmanned pumps 24/7.
